Spaceman

Instructions

  1. Press "Play Now" to begin
  2. Guess a letter to fill in the blank of the mystery word
  3. If player guesses correctly, a blank space will be filled with the letter
  4. Incorrect guesses will cause a part of the spaceship to be drawn
  5. After seven incorrect guesses, the player loses
  6. Player wins by correctly guessing all the letters to the mystery word before getting seven incorrect
